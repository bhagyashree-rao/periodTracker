WHY USE SPOT?

We at SPOT understand the troubles of managing your period cycles. There may be a case where you might be going camping, or to a party, or work or anywhere outside your home. You might not be prepared and have sanitary napkins with you. SPOT helps you in solving this problem with the help of a period predictor which does the job of predicting when your period might be coming depending on when you got it the previous month.
